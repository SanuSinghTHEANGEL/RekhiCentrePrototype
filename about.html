ABOUT US

The Rekhi Centre of Excellence for the Science of Happiness was established at Indian Institute of Technology(IIT) Kharagpur, in collaboration with the Rekhi Foundation for Happiness. Inspired by the vision of Dr. Satinder Singh Rekhi and Mrs. Harpreet rekhi in 2016, the Centre was founded with the belief that emotional well-being and inner happiness are as vital as technical and academic excellence in the life of an engineer. The initiative is part of a larger national movement led by the Rekhi Foundation to bring the science of happiness into higher education institutions across India. SGSITS proudly became a part of this movement in 2025, becoming one of the first technical institutions in Madhya Pradesh to formally adopt this transformative concept. Since its inception, the Centre has conducted workshops, talks, experiential sessions, and mindfulness activities to promote emotional resilience and mental well-being among students and faculty

VISION

To nurture a compassionate, resilient, and

emotionally intelligent academic community

by promoting the science and practice of happiness.

MISSION

To introduce structured programs and workshops focused on mental well-being, mindfulness, gratitude, and life purpose. To encourage research and innovation in the domain of emotional wellness and positive psychology. To create a campus culture where happiness is not just an emotion but a way of life.

KEY ACTIVITIES

Happiness Courses & Modules: Offering elective and value-added courses on happiness, stress management, and self-awareness.

Workshops & Seminars: Regularly hosting sessions on mindfulness, meditation, resilience, and values-based living.

Research & Collaboration: Supporting interdisciplinary research on the science of happiness and its practical applications in education and work-life.

Campus Engagement: Initiating student clubs, gratitude circles, and well-being campaigns to foster a happier, more connected student environment.

WHY IT MATTERS AT SGSITS

At SGSITS, we believe that true engineering is not only about machines and code—it is about building better lives and a better society. Through the Rekhi Centre, we aim to empower our students and faculty with the inner tools needed to face life’s challenges with clarity, calmness, and compassion.

JOIN THE MOVEMENT

We invite every member of the SGSITS family—students, teachers, staff, and alumni—to engage with the Rekhi Centre’s initiatives and embark on a journey toward deeper happiness, mindful living, and meaningful success.
