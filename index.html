About the Centre

The Rekhi Centre of Excellence for the Science of Happiness aims to foster well-being and holistic development through scientific understanding and practice of happiness. The Centre is established in collaboration with SGSITS Indore and is inspired by similar initiatives at IIT Kharagpur, IIM Lucknow, and other premier institutions.

Our focus is on cultivating inner well-being through research, education, and public engagement. The Centre serves as a hub for exploring emotional and psychological resilience, personal growth, and the role of happiness in education and workspaces. We believe that happiness is not just a feeling but a learnable skill that can transform individuals and institutions alike.

Through interdisciplinary collaboration, the Centre brings together experts from psychology, philosophy, management, and technology to create a scientifically grounded and culturally sensitive approach to happiness. By embedding positive practices into the fabric of institutional life, we aim to build a community where mental wellness, purpose, and connection thrive.

Objectives

  • To promote research and education in the science of happiness.
  • To conduct workshops and seminars on emotional well-being.
  • To integrate happiness-focused modules into existing academic curriculum..
  • To create awareness about mental health and well-being.

Programs & Courses

The centre will offer various workshops, certificate programs, and elective courses that focus on emotional intelligence, mindfulness, gratitude, and resilience. These are open to students, faculty, and professionals.

Events & Activities

  • Monthly Happiness Talks by Experts
  • Meditation and Mindfulness Sessions
  • Annual Happiness Fest
  • Student-Led Happiness Initiatives
